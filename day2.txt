DAY 2 VIM WORKOUT: PRECISION & OBJECTS (25 MIN)

INSTRUCTIONS:

Open this file: vim day2.txt

Follow the steps. No arrow keys!

Use 'u' to undo and 'Ctrl-r' to redo.

SECTION 1: SEARCH NAVIGATION (5 MIN)

Goal: Use search to jump across the screen instantly.

DRILL A: Word Search under Cursor ()
Instruction: Put cursor on the word 'BANANA'. Press * to find the next one.
Press 'n' to go to the next match, and 'N' for the previous.
Target: Find every BANANA: BANANA, apple, orange, BANANA, grape, BANANA.

DRILL B: Manual Search (/)
Instruction: Press '/', type 'target', and press <Enter>.
Target: This line is just filler.
Target: You want to land on this target.

SECTION 2: THE "A" VS "I" TEXT OBJECTS (5 MIN)

Goal: Understand the difference between "Inner" (i) and "Around" (a).

DRILL A: Delete Inner Word (diw)
Instruction: Put cursor on 'REDUNDANT'. Press 'diw'.
Note: This deletes the word but leaves the spaces.
Target: This is a REDUNDANT word.

DRILL B: Delete Around Word (daw)
Instruction: Put cursor on 'EXTRA'. Press 'daw'.
Note: This deletes the word AND the surrounding space.
Target: Remove the EXTRA word.

SECTION 3: VISUAL MODE TRICKS (5 MIN)

Goal: Mastering selection and switching sides.

DRILL A: Visual Selection + 'o' (Other side)
Instruction:

Put cursor on 'Start'. Press 'v'.

Move to 'End' using 'w'.

Press 'o'. Your cursor jumps back to 'Start' while keeping the selection!

Expand the selection backwards, then press 'd' to delete.
Target: [Start] --- some text in the middle --- [End]

SECTION 4: DELETE UNTIL/FIND (5 MIN)

Goal: Combine operators with search motions.

DRILL A: Delete Until (dt)
Instruction: Put cursor on the 'X'. Press 'dt' then '.'.
Target: X Delete everything until the period. This stays.

DRILL B: Change Find (cf)
Instruction: Put cursor on 'C'. Press 'cf' then 'X'. Type 'DONE'.
Target: Change this text X but stop after the X.

SECTION 5: MACRO WITH APPENDING (5 MIN)

Goal: Learning to build onto a macro.

DRILL A: The Bullet List
Instruction:

Put cursor on 'Alpha'.

Press 'qa' to record. Press 'I', type '- ', <ESC>, 'j', 'q'.

Now, we want to add a period to the end of each.

Put cursor back on 'Alpha'.

Press 'qA' (Capital A appends to macro 'a').

Press 'k' (up), 'A', type '.', <ESC>, 'j', 'q'.

Run '3@a' on the remaining lines.
