============================================================================== VIM INTERACTIVE PRACTICE LAB (20-30 MIN)

INSTRUCTIONS:

Open this file in Vim: vim day0.txt

Follow the steps in each section.

Do NOT use your arrow keys.

If you get stuck in a mode, press <ESC> to return to Normal Mode.

SECTION 1: THE BASICS OF MOVEMENT (5 MIN) Goal: Stop using 'h' 'j' 'k' 'l' for every character.

DRILL A: Word Jumps
Instruction: Use 'w' to jump to the start of words and 'b' to jump back.
Target: Move from word to word until you reach the [TARGET].

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy [TARGET] dog.

DRILL B: End of Word
Instruction: Use 'e' to jump to the END of the word.
Target: Land exactly on the '!' at the end of each word.

Jump! To! Each! Exclamation! Point!

DRILL C: Line Extremes
Instruction: Press '0' (zero) to jump to the start of the line.
Press '$' to jump to the very end of the line.

[START] ----------------------------------------------------------------- [END]

SECTION 2: CHARACTER SEARCH NAVIGATION (5 MIN) Goal: Use 'f' (find) and 't' (until) for rapid horizontal movement.

DRILL A: Forward Find
Instruction: Press 'f' followed by the character 'X'.
Repeat by pressing ';' (semicolon) to find the next one.
Target: Find every 'X' in this line.

There is an X here, an X there, and an X way over here.

DRILL B: Find Until
Instruction: Press 't' followed by ')' to land just BEFORE the parenthesis.
Target: Land on the '8' in (98).

Look at this number: (98).

SECTION 3: EDITING GRAMMAR (OPERATOR + MOTION) (10 MIN) Goal: Combine 'd' (delete), 'c' (change), or 'y' (yank) with motions.

DRILL A: Delete Word (dw)
Instruction: Put cursor on 'REDUNDANT'. Press 'dw'.
Target: Clean up this sentence.

This sentence has a REDUNDANT word inside of it.

DRILL B: Change Inner Word (ciw)
Instruction: Put cursor anywhere inside 'ERROR'. Press 'ciw'.
Type 'SUCCESS' and press <ESC>.

The status of the operation is: ERROR.

DRILL C: Delete to End of Line (d$)
Instruction: Move to the 'S' in 'STAY'. Press 'd$'.
Target: Remove the extra words.

This part should stay. STAY BUT DELETE EVERYTHING AFTER THIS.

SECTION 4: TEXT OBJECTS (THE MAGIC OF VIM) (5 MIN) Goal: Master 'i' (inner) and 'a' (around) objects.

DRILL A: Change Inner Quotes (ci")
Instruction: Put cursor inside the quotes. Press 'ci"'.
Type 'Hello World' and press <ESC>.

The computer said: "CHANGE ME TO SOMETHING ELSE"

DRILL B: Delete Inside Brackets (di[)
Instruction: Put cursor inside the brackets. Press 'di['.

List of items: [Apples, Oranges, Pears, Bananas, Grapes]

Instruction: 1. Put cursor on the 'L' in 'Line 1'.
2. Press 'Ctrl+v' (Visual Block mode).
3. Use 'j' to select all four lines downward.
4. Press 'Shift+i' (Capital I for insert).
5. Type 'DONE: '
6. Press <ESC>. Wait 1 second for the change to apply to all lines.
