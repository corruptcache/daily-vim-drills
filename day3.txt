==============================================================================

DAY 3 VIM WORKOUT: POWER EDITING (25 MIN)

==============================================================================

INSTRUCTIONS:

Open this file: day3.txt

Perform the tasks in order.

No arrow keys. Use 'u' to undo any mistakes.

SECTION 1: THE LONG JUMP (5 MIN)

Goal: Rapid vertical navigation across the entire file.

DRILL A: Start and End
Instruction:

Press 'gg' to jump to the very first line of the file.

Press 'G' (Shift+g) to jump to the very last line of the file.

Use ':25' followed by <Enter> to jump specifically to line 25.

DRILL B: Screen Positioning
Instruction:

Press 'H' (High) to jump to the top of the visible screen.

Press 'M' (Middle) to jump to the middle of the screen.

Press 'L' (Low) to jump to the bottom of the screen.

Press 'zz' to center the current line on your screen.

DRILL C: Matching Pairs (%)
Instruction: Put your cursor on the first '[' below and press '%'.
Target: [ This is a long bracketed sentence that spans (multiple) parts. ]

SECTION 2: REGISTERS & THE CLIPBOARD (5 MIN)

Goal: Use specific registers so you don't lose text when deleting.

DRILL A: Yanking to a Named Register
Instruction:

Put cursor on 'SECRET'.

Press '"ayiw' (Double-quote, a, yank inner word). This saves 'SECRET' to register 'a'.

Move to the next line. Press 'diw' to delete 'DELETE_ME'.

Press '"ap' to paste 'SECRET' from register 'a'.
Target 1: SECRET
Target 2: DELETE_ME

SECTION 3: LINE MANIPULATION (5 MIN)

Goal: Cleaning up formatting with 'J' and ':sort'.

DRILL A: Joining Lines (J)
Instruction: Put cursor on 'Line 1'. Press 'J' (Shift+j) repeatedly.
Target:
Line 1
Line 2
Line 3
Line 4

DRILL B: Sorting Lines (:sort)
Instruction:

Highlight the list below using 'V' (Visual Line mode).

Type ':sort' and press <Enter>.
Target:
Zebra
Apple
Monkey
Banana

SECTION 4: GLOBAL SUBSTITUTE (5 MIN)

Goal: Changing text across multiple lines at once.

DRILL A: Search and Replace with Confirmation
Instruction:

Type ':%s/CHERRY/BERRY/gc' and press <Enter>.

Press 'y' to confirm each change.
Target:
I have a CHERRY.
You have a CHERRY.
We all have a CHERRY.

SECTION 5: THE GLOBAL COMMAND (5 MIN)

Goal: Perform actions on lines that match a pattern.

DRILL A: Delete lines containing 'TRASH'
Instruction: Type ':g/TRASH/d' and press <Enter>.
Target:
Keep this line.
TRASH - Remove this.
Keep this too.
TRASH - This should go.

==============================================================================

WORKOUT COMPLETE

==============================================================================

Tip: Use ':wq' to save and quit, or ':q!' to exit without saving so you
can repeat these drills later!
